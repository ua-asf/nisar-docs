# Webinars

## NISAR Science Team Meeting

Leaders of the NISAR project held a [virtual town hall and data access webinar](https://www.earthdata.nasa.gov/learn/webinars/nisar-science-team-town-hall-data-access-webinar) during their Science Team meeting in February 2026. The town hall offers updates on the mission status, science, data products, and upcoming data releases. The data access webinar provides information and resources for accessing and analyzing NISAR data.
