# Earthdata Forum

The [Earthdata Forum](https://forum.earthdata.nasa.gov/) is the best venue for communication on topics involving NASA Earth Science data. 

Subject-matter experts answer questions approved by forum moderators. 

Tags for the following categories are assigned to all topics in the forum: *Discipline*, *DAAC*, *Project*, and *Services/Usage*. These tags can be used to filter the topics, and tags specific to the NISAR mission have been added. 

Users may want to pay particular attention to *ASF* posts tagged with *Announcements*, *Data Recipes*, and *FAQ*.
